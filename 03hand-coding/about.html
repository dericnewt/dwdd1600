What started as a simple Excel spreadsheet has now grown to be an expansive collection of sports data and highlights supported by the best community in the world!

We began with simple roots when a student at the University of Utah came across several databases of statistics and info on various college football programs, but was unable to find any real flexibility or user-friendly features that made analyzing the data simple and clear. Taking matters into their own hands, they spent months data-scraping and collecting everything they could find and compiling it into a customizable spreadsheet that displayed the data in a unique way.

The spreadsheet would eventually spring to life the C.F.B.B.S.A.M. (College Football Blue Bloods Statistical Analyzer Matrix), or S.A.M. for short, which eventually gave way to an Instagram community, which grew into the nation-wide outlet that we are today!
